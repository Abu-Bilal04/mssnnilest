About MSSN NILEST Branch

The Muslim Students Society of Nigeria (MSSN), NILEST Branch, is a community of devoted Muslim students committed to fostering unity, spiritual growth, and academic excellence within the Nigerian Institute of Leather and Science Technology.

Our Mission

To promote Islamic values, encourage moral uprightness, and support members in their academic and personal pursuits.

Our Vision

To raise a generation of knowledgeable, responsible, and God-conscious youths who will positively impact society.

Our Services

We strive to nurture faith, education, and community through impactful programs and initiatives that strengthen our members spiritually, academically, and socially.

Spiritual Development

Organizing Jumu’ah prayers, Tafsir sessions, and Islamic lectures to enrich members’ spiritual lives.

Mentorship & Guidance

Providing mentorship programs, counseling, and academic guidance to help members excel in both faith and education.

Community Outreach

Engaging in charitable activities, community service, and interfaith dialogue to promote peace and understanding.
